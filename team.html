Coach Chaz is the founder and head trainer at NDO Fitness — Nova Scotia's first private basketball facility. With years of playing and coaching experience, Chaz built NDO with one mission: to give athletes of all ages a dedicated space to develop their craft under expert guidance.

His coaching philosophy is built on patience, progressive skill-building, and a deep connection with each athlete. Whether it's a 3-year-old Mini Baller or a U16 player preparing for club tryouts, Coach Chaz brings the same energy and attention to every session.

Our Philosophy

More than
a saying.

Three principles that define everything we do at NDO Fitness.

Excellence

We hold every athlete to a high standard — not because we want to pressure them, but because we believe in what they're capable of. Every session is a chance to get better.

Community

NDO is more than a training facility. It's a place where athletes build friendships, develop character, and become part of something bigger than any single skill or score.

Progress

Growth isn't always linear, but it's always the goal. We track, measure, and celebrate every milestone — because the journey of development is as important as the destination.
