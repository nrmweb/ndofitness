Quick Answers

Common
Questions.

What should I bring to my first session?
Athletic wear, clean indoor court shoes, and a water bottle. All basketball equipment is provided at NDO Fitness.
How do I cancel or reschedule?
Email us at ndoleague@gmail.com at least 24 hours before your session. Last-minute cancellations may be subject to a fee.
Is there parking at the facility?
Yes — free parking is available on site at 286 Bluewater Road. No time limits.
Do you offer trial sessions?
Yes! First-time athletes are welcome to book a standard individual session as a trial before committing to a program or package.
What age groups do you train?
We train athletes from age 3 all the way to adult. Our programs are segmented by age — Mini Ballers (3–5), U10, U13, and U16.
How do I join a sold-out program?
Send us an email to join the waitlist. We run multiple blocks each season and will reach out as spots open up.

Still have questions?

Email us at ndoleague@gmail.com — we're always happy to help.
