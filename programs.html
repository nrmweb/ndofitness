Program FAQ.

What should athletes bring to sessions?
Athletic wear, clean indoor court shoes, and a water bottle. All basketball equipment is provided at the facility.
What happens if we miss a session?
We understand life happens. Missed sessions can be made up based on availability — just reach out to us at ndoleague@gmail.com to arrange a makeup.
Are the programs competitive or recreational?
Both! Every program is structured to challenge athletes at their level. U10 and Mini Ballers focus on fun and fundamentals, while U13 and U16 introduce more competitive elements.
How do I register?
Click the Register button on any program card above and fill out the booking form. We'll confirm your registration within 24 hours and send you details for the first session.
Is there a waitlist for sold-out programs?
Yes — if a program shows Sold Out, you can join the waitlist via the Contact page. We run multiple session blocks each season.
What age is best to start?
There's no wrong time to start! Our Mini Ballers program starts at age 3, and we've welcomed first-time athletes at every level. Coach Chaz meets every athlete where they are.
